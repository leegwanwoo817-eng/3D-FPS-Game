# Web Version - 3D FPS Game

## How to Play

### Online
Open `index.html` in your browser:
1. Download or clone the repository
2. Open `index.html` directly in your browser
3. Click on the game window to start

### Controls
- **WASD** - Move forward, left, backward, right
- **Mouse** - Look around (move mouse to aim)
- **Left Click** - Shoot enemies
- **Space** - Jump
- **ESC** - Release mouse lock

### Objective
- Kill all the red enemy boxes
- Don't let them reach you (they deal 10 damage per hit)
- Survive as long as possible
- Get the highest score!

## Game Mechanics

### Shooting
- Start with 30 ammo
- Each shot uses 1 ammo
- Ammo refills when you kill enemies (spawn rate increases)
- Cooldown between shots: 200ms

### Enemies
- Red cubes that chase you
- Deal 10 damage per attack
- Give 100 points when killed
- Spawn randomly around you

### Health
- Start with 100 health
- Game ends when health reaches 0
- Health bar in top-left corner

## Tips
- Keep moving to avoid enemy attacks
- Use the terrain to your advantage
- Focus on one enemy at a time
- Jump to avoid melee attacks
- Watch your ammo count

## Features
- 3D graphics with Three.js

- Score tracking
- Enemy AI with pathfinding
